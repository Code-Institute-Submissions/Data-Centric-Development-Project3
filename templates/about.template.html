Welome to LogIt

Saw something amazing during your dive and want to keep a log on it? Introducing LogIt, the online Dive Logger application created by Divers for Divers.


Access LogIt from your computer, mobile and keep your dive logs online. Upload images of your sightings and share your dive logs to your friends!


Check out our resident Divers, John and Jane. Go to "Profile" and enter their emails - "JohnDoe@testing.com" and "JaneDoe@testing.com". Click on the latest sightings, to see all the sightings spotted for that particular species!
